Fish Tacos

Ingredients:

For the Fish:
- 1.5 lb tilapia
- 0.5 tsp ground cumin
- 0.5 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp black pepper
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 Tbsp unsalted butter

For Assembly:
- 24 small white corn tortillas
- 0.5 small purple cabbage
- 2 medium avocados (sliced)
- 2 roma tomatoes (diced, optional)
- 0.5 diced red onion
- 0.5 bunch cilantro
- 4 oz cotija cheese (grated)
- 1 lime (cut into 8 wedges)

For Taco Sauce:
- 0.5 cup sour cream
- 0.33 cup mayonnaise
- 2 Tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp sriracha sauce

Instructions:

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Mix seasonings and sprinkle evenly over both sides of tilapia.

Drizzle fish with olive oil and dot with butter. Bake at 375Â°F for 20-25 minutes. Optional: broil 3-5 minutes to brown edges.

Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl and whisk until blended.

Toast corn tortillas on a skillet over medium-high heat.

Assemble tacos with fish, remaining ingredients, cotija cheese, sauce, and lime wedge.
